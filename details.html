Wedding Details

The Venue

8498 Seaview Pl NW, Seattle, WA 98117

Golden Gardens is a beautiful beach park in northwest Seattle. The ceremony will be at the large brick bathhouse. Please drive or Lyft to the venue, as the road down to the beach is not pedestrian friendly. There is plenty of free parking at the bathhouse, but we encourage carpooling and rideshares!

The Ceremony and Reception

Please arrive at the venue at 5pm.

Cocktail attire. We like to get a little fancy.

Happy hour and casual dinner reception following the ceremony.

Dancing and festivities until we wrap up the evening at 10pm.
