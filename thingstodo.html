Things to do in Seattle

Downtown Seattle

  • Explore Pike Place Market
    • Put some gum on the Gum Wall
    • See an improv show at Unexpected Productions
    • Eat a delicious pastry at Piroshky Piroshky
    • Look for our Cohnobi Pack market charm
    • Get a venti at the oldest (not original) Starbucks
  • Ask us for a Columbia Tower Tour (highest view in town!)
  • Smith Tower Observation Deck
  • Seattle Underground Tour
  • Seattle Central Library (simply an architectural wonder) and Chocolati (great coffee and hot chocolate, inside the library!)
  • Seattle Art Museum
  • Seattle Aquarium
  • Kerry Park (great view of the Seattle skyline)
  • Seattle Center
    • Space Needle
    • Museum of Pop Culture (science fiction and music)
    • Pacific Science Center
  • Great restaurants (and happy hours!)
    • Umisake house
    • List
    • Shiro’s sushi
    • Serious Pie
    • Artisan Cafe (for a quick morning bite in Belltown)
    • Mama’s Mexican Kitchen
    • Local 360
  • Bars and speakeasies
    • Bathtub Gin
    • Ohana belltown (tiki bar)
    • Deep Dive (speakeasy under Amazon Sphere’s - it’s definitely overpriced but it’s worth the experience. Reservations highly recommended)

Ballard

  • Ballard Locks and Gardens
  • Great restaurants and bars
    • Bitterroot
    • Hazlewood (speakeasy)
    • Cafe Mox (board-game cafe)
    • The Walrus and the Carpenter
    • Senor Moose
    • Percy's & Co. Seattle
    • Noble Fir
  • Coffee and Tea
    • Ballard Coffee Works
    • Miro Tea
  • Nordic Museum
  • Salt and Straw ice-cream
  • Ballard Farmer’s Market

Fremont

  • Fremont Troll
  • Explore downtown Fremont: great cafe’s, coffee shops, art stores
  • Theo’s Chocolate Factory Tours
  • Great restaurants and bars
    • Cafe Turko
    • El Camino
    • Brouwers
    • Add-a-ball (pin-ball bar, great late night hangout)
    • Fremont Brewery
  • Fremont Coffee Company

Wallingford/University District

  • Greenlake Park
  • Woodland Park Zoo
  • Gas Works Park
  • Archie McPhee’s (ridiculous toy/gimmick store)
  • Great restaurants and bars
    • Octopus bar
    • Rancho Bravo
    • Dick’s Drive-in (classic Seattle drive thru burger joint)
    • Burgundian
    • Bongo’s (jamaican food)
    • Taste of India
  • Molly Moon’s ice-cream
  • Cafe Allegro (oldest coffee shop in Seattle)
  • The Japanese Cherry Blossoms at University of Washington. They’re part of the same group that was donated to Washington DC!

If you’re planning on an extended stay...

  • Take a ferry from Downtown Seattle to Bremerton
    • Take a tour of the USS Turner Joy, a Naval Destroyer-turned-museum
    • Breathe some fresh ocean air and maybe see some whales on your one hour ferry ride
  • Go on a hike!
    • Rattlesnake Ridge
    • Discovery Park
    • Schmitz Preserve Park (an old growth forest inside Seattle city limits!)
    • Arboretum
    • Snoqualmie Falls
  • Leavenworth (Bavarian/German town)
